Vegetable Consommé with Edible Flower Ravioli

Chef: Alain Passard
Source: adapted from Arpège

A crystal-clear vegetable consommé served with delicate ravioli filled with ricotta and edible flowers, creating a visually stunning and aromatic dish.

Ingredients

  • Ravioli (see sub-recipe)
  • Vegetable consommé (see sub-recipe)
  • Edible flower petals (for garnish)
  • few drops Chive oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place ravioli in the center of a shallow bowl.
  2. Gently pour hot vegetable consommé around the ravioli.
  3. Garnish with flower petals and a drizzle of chive oil.
  4. Serve immediately for a delicate and aromatic presentation.
