Deconstructed Chocolate Chip Cookie Sphere
Source: adapted from Alinea
Inspiring Chef: Grant Achatz

A deconstructed chocolate chip cookie in a crisp chocolate sphere with a molten or ganache liquid center. Served with optional cocoa, chocolate drizzle, or ice cream.

Serves 4–6

  • Cookie Dough Ball or Ganache (Choose one for the center of the sphere. See sub-recipe)

Steps:

  1. 1.Place chocolate hemispheres on a warm baking sheet to slightly melt edge.
  2. 2.Place a cookie dough ball or ganache inside one chocolate hemisphere.
  3. 3.Top with the matching hemisphere to form a complete sphere.
  4. 4.Chill briefly to set.
  5. 5.Plate: crack the sphere with a knife at the table for the deconstructed effect.
  6. 6.Optional plating: dust with cocoa powder, drizzle with chocolate sauce, or add a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream or caramel foam. Or all of them!

Sub-Recipes:

Chocolate Sphere Shell
Ingredients:
  • 200 g – High-quality dark chocolate (60–65%)
  • 4 – Silicone hemisphere molds
Steps:
  1. 1.Bring water to a simmer in a medium saucepan.
  2. 2.Place a glass or metal bowl on top of the saucepan.
  3. 3.Add about 75 g of the dark chocolate and melt until the temperature is 120°F (50°C).
  4. 4.Add in 25 g of chocolate, stirring contantly.
  5. 5.Pour tempered chocolate into silicone molds, coating evenly.
  6. 6.Scrape excess chocolate, refrigerate 5–10 minutes, then repeat steps 3 and 4 for a second layer for sturdiness.
  7. 7.Chill until firm, then carefully remove the chocolate hemispheres.
Cookie Dough Filling
Ingredients:
  • 100 g – Unsalted butter (softened)
  • 80 g – Brown sugar
  • 40 g – Granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp – Vanilla extract
  • 1 – Egg yolk
  • 150 g – All-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp – Baking soda
  • pinch – Salt
  • 100 g – Chocolate chips
Steps:
  1. 1.Cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  2. 2.Mix in vanilla and egg yolk.
  3. 3.Sift in flour, baking soda, and salt; fold to combine.
  4. 4.Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. 5.Roll into small balls that fit inside the chocolate hemispheres.
  6. 6.Chill dough for at least 30 minutes to maintain shape during baking.
Liquid Center - Eggless Ganache Core Option
Ingredients:
  • 100 g – White or milk chocolate
  • 50 mL – Cream
  • 1 tsp – Liqueur (optional, like Baileys or coffee liqueur)
Steps:

  2. 2.Chill slightly to thicken but remain pourable.

    Caramel Foam
    Yield: 1 cup
    Ingredients:
    • 1 cup – Sugar
    • 1 cup – Water
    • 1/2 teaspoon – Vanilla extract
    • 3 tablespoons – Heave Whipping Cream
    • 2 tablespoons – Whole milk
    • Pinch – Sea Salt
    Steps:
    1. 1.Combine sugar and 1/2 cup of water in a small saucepan over medium-high heat.
    2. 2.Let simmer for 18-20 minutes or until it turns a light amber color. Do not stir.
    3. 3.In a separate saucepan, boil the other 1/2 cup of water.
    4. 4.Once the amber color is achieved, carefully pour in the boiling water, whisking constantly.
    5. 5.Whisk in vanilla extract.
    6. 6.Let cool.
    7. 7.Combine the heavy cream, milk and sea salt with the cooled caramel syrup.
    8. 8.Using the foamer, spray directly onto the cookie.
