Dirty Vege-Burger with Smoky Mushroom Demi-Glace and Fermented Aioli

Chef:
Source:

An elevated 'dirty' vegetable burger featuring a deeply roasted mushroom-quinoa patty with crisp crust, glossy dripping smoky mushroom demi-glace, fermented aioli, and refined toppings for indulgent yet refined street-food luxury.

Ingredients

  • 350g Mixed mushrooms (shiitake, oyster, chestnut) (300g for patty + extra 50g for demi-glace, finely chopped)
  • 1 large Red onion (finely diced)
  • 3 Garlic cloves (2 minced for patty, 1 for demi-glace)
  • 1 medium Carrot (grated)
  • 150g Cooked quinoa
  • 400g can Black beans (drained and roughly mashed, reserve 2 tbsp liquid)
  • 50g Toasted walnuts (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp White miso paste
  • 1 tbsp Tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp flax + 5 tbsp water Flaxseed meal + water (binder)
  • 1 tsp each paprika & cumin, ½ tsp coriander Smoked paprika, cumin, coriander
  • as needed Olive oil, sea salt, black pepper
  • Smoky Mushroom Demi-Glace (reduced roasting juices and extra mushrooms (see sub-recipe))
  • Fermented garlic aioli (vegan mayo base + kimchi brine)
  • 1 Red onions for pickling (quick-pickled)
  • for crispy frying Shallots
  • Ripe avocado, heirloom tomato, microgreens, edible flower (for topping)
  • 4 Vegan brioche buns

Instructions

  1. Weigh all ingredients precisely using the digital kitchen scale.
  2. Toss 300g mushrooms, onion, 2 garlic cloves, and carrot with olive oil, salt, pepper, and spices. Roast at 200°C (400°F) for 20–25 minutes until deeply caramelized. Reserve roasting juices.
  3. Pulse the roasted vegetables with mashed beans, quinoa, walnuts, miso, tomato paste, and flax binder in a high-powered food processor until cohesive but textured. Chill 30 minutes.
  4. Divide mixture into four equal portions (~180–200g each) by weight. Use the burger patty press to form uniform 2cm-thick patties.
  5. Heat a cast-iron pan very hot with oil. Use the fish spatula to place and flip patties. Sear 4–5 minutes per side to develop a deep crust. Check internal temperature with the precision digital thermometer (target 70–75°C / 160–165°F).
  6. Use the mandoline slicer (1–2mm setting) for paper-thin red onions (quick-pickle in rice vinegar, sugar, salt) and shallots (fry until golden and crispy).
  7. Prepare the Smoky Mushroom Demi-Glace (see sub-recipe). Toast buns.
  8. Assemble: spread fermented aioli on bottom bun, add seared patty, spoon smoky mushroom demi-glace generously so it drips artfully, layer avocado, tomato, pickled onions, crispy shallots, microgreens, and edible flower.
  9. Plate on a pristine white dish with an artistic sauce smear and scattered microgreens.
