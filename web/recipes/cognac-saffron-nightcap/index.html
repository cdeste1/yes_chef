Cognac & Saffron Nightcap

Chef:
Source: Yes Chef! Kitchen

A deep, aromatic cocktail of XO cognac, saffron honey, and smoked vanilla bitters.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces XO Cognac
  • Saffron Honey (see sub-recipe)
  • 2-3 dashes Orange Bitters

Instructions

  1. Stir cognac with saffron honey and bitters over ice.
  2. Strain into a warmed snifter.
