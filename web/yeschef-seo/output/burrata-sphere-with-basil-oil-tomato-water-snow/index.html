Burrata Sphere with Basil Oil & Tomato Water Snow

Chef: Massimo Bottura
Source: adapted from Modern Italian

A delicate burrata sphere paired with aromatic basil oil and refreshing tomato water snow, combining creamy, herbal, and acidic elements.

Ingredients

  • Burrata Sphere (see sub-recipe)
  • Basil Oil (see sub-recipe)
  • Tomato Water Snow (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Spoon a small pool of basil oil on the plate. Place the burrata sphere in the center.
  2. Grate tomato water snow over and around the sphere right before serving.
  3. Garnish with micro basil leaves, cracked black pepper, or a drizzle of olive oil. Serve immediately.
