Five Ages of Parmigiano Reggiano
Source: adapted from Osteria Francescana (Modena, Italy)
Inspiring Chef: Massimo Bottura

A tribute to Emilia-Romagna’s king of cheeses, presenting Parmigiano Reggiano in five distinct textures and maturations — crisp, sauce, foam, wafer, and air.

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • Black pepper oil (for garnish)
  • Micro basil (for garnish)
  • Parmigiano Crisp (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Sauce (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Foam (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Wafer (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Air (see sub-recipe)

Steps:

  1. 1.Spoon warm sauce in center of a shallow bowl.
  2. 2.Add Parmigiano foam, crisp, and wafer.
  3. 3.Top with Parmigiano air.
  4. 4.Finish with micro basil and a drizzle of black pepper oil.

Sub-Recipes:

Parmigiano Crisp (24-month aged)
Ingredients:
  • 50 g – Parmigiano Reggiano (24-month aged) (finely grated)
Steps:
  1. 1.Preheat oven to 350°F. (175°C)
  2. 2.Spread cheese into thin circles on a silicone mat.
  3. 3.Bake 4–5 minutes until golden and crisp.
  4. 4.Cool on a rack before use.
Warm Parmigiano Sauce (30-month aged)
Ingredients:
  • 100 g – Parmigiano Reggiano (30-month aged)
  • 200 mL – Milk
  • 50 mL – Cream
Steps:
  1. 1.Simmer milk and cream together.
  2. 2.Add grated cheese, stir until melted.
  3. 3.Blend and strain for a smooth emulsion.
Parmigiano Foam (36-month aged)
Ingredients:
  • 150 mL – Parmigiano sauce (from above)
  • 1 – Gelatin sheet (soaked)
Steps:
  1. 1.Dissolve gelatin in warm Parmigiano sauce.
  2. 2.Strain and transfer to foamer.
  3. 3.Spray foam onto a baking sheet and keep warm until service.
Parmigiano Wafer (18-month aged)
Ingredients:
  • 50 g – Parmigiano Reggiano (18-month aged)
  • 25 g – Flour
  • 50 mL – Water
Steps:
  1. 1.Mix ingredients into a thin batter.
  2. 2.Spread thin on a baking sheet and bake until crisp.
Parmigiano Air (48-month aged)
Ingredients:
  • 100 mL – Parmigiano stock (steep cheese in hot water)
  • 1 g – Soy lecithin
Steps:
  1. 1.Blend stock with soy lecithin.
  2. 2.Froth with immersion blender until airy foam forms.
