Avocado Royale

Chef:
Source:

An elegant avocado-centric brunch course featuring vacuum-compressed Hass avocado carpaccio, sweet Dungeness crab, a custardy 63°C sous-vide quail egg, and a light truffle-infused avocado espuma.

Ingredients

  • 2 large Ripe Hass avocados
  • 100 g Fresh Dungeness crab meat (cooked and picked)
  • 4 Quail eggs (very fresh, room temperature)
  • 30 g Fresh black truffle
  • 2 large Egg yolks (for espuma base)
  • 150 g Neutral oil or clarified butter (warm)
  • 1 tsp Yuzu kosho (or fresh yuzu zest + pinch of chili)
  • 1 Fresh yuzu juice (or Meyer lemon)
  • 20 g Ossetra caviar
  • as needed Micro shiso or chervil leaves
  • as needed Edible 24K gold leaf
  • 1 tsp Yuzu gel or reduction
  • to season Fine sea salt and white pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare the compressed avocado carpaccio and avocado-truffle espuma
  2. Sous-vide the quail eggs
  3. Gently warm the Dungeness crab meat with a touch of yuzu juice and salt. Do not overcook.
  4. Peel the sous-vide quail eggs carefully just before plating.
  5. Season components lightly with salt and white pepper as needed.
  6. Plating Layer 1: Place the compressed avocado carpaccio disc in the center of the plate using a 3-inch ring mold.
  7. Plating Layer 2: Add a thin even layer of warm crab meat on the avocado base.
  8. Plating Layer 3: Carefully place one peeled sous-vide quail egg on top of the crab. Remove the ring mold.
  9. Dispense generous clouds of avocado-truffle espuma over and around the egg with the iSi siphon.
  10. Spoon Ossetra caviar in an elegant line across the espuma. Shave remaining fresh black truffle generously over the plate.
  11. Finish with tiny dots of yuzu gel, micro shiso leaves, and delicate flakes of edible gold leaf applied with micro tweezers.
