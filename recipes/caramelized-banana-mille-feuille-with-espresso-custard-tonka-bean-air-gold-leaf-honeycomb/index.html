Caramelized Banana Mille-Feuille with Espresso Custard, Tonka Bean Air & Gold Leaf Honeycomb

A Michelin-level brunch dessert featuring vacuum-compressed caramelized banana layers stacked with espresso custard sheets, paired with tonka bean air, gold-leaf honeycomb shards, and aromatic banana essence.

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • 6 – ripe bananas (firm but sweet)
  • 150g – superfine sugar
  • 50g – glucose syrup
  • 20g – unsalted butter
  • 1g – sea salt
  • 300ml – heavy cream
  • 100ml – whole milk
  • 5 – egg yolks
  • 80g – sugar (for custard)
  • 20g – finely ground espresso
  • 2g – agar-agar
  • 250ml – whole milk (for tonka air)
  • 1 – tonka bean (microplaned)
  • 2g – soy lecithin
  • 200g – sugar (for honeycomb)
  • 60g – honey
  • 50ml – water
  • 8g – baking soda
  • to finish – edible gold leaf
  • to garnish – cocoa nibs (crushed)
  • to garnish – micro mint
  • to garnish – espresso reduction (in droplets)

Steps:

  1. 1.Prepare vacuum-compressed bananas: Slice bananas into thin planks and seal in a vacuum chamber at full pressure to intensify flavor and density. Rest 10 minutes.
  2. 2.Caramelize banana layers: Heat sugar and glucose to 170°C (338°F). Add butter and salt, then coat banana slices. Lay flat and lightly torch for a glossy finish.
  3. 3.Prepare espresso custard: Heat cream, milk, and espresso. Temper into egg yolks and sugar. Cook to 82°C (180°F), whisk in agar, and set in thin sheets. Chill and cut into precise layers.
  4. 4.Create honeycomb: Cook sugar, honey, and water to 150°C (302°F). Remove from heat, whisk in baking soda, and pour onto a silicone mat. Cool, break into shards, and apply gold leaf.
  5. 5.Prepare tonka bean air: Infuse warm milk with tonka bean, strain, and add lecithin. Use an ultrasonic homogenizer to generate a stable, fine foam.
  6. 6.Optional banana crisp: Flash freeze thin banana slices in liquid nitrogen and shatter into delicate shards.
  7. 7.Plating: Stack alternating banana and custard layers into a mille-feuille. Arrange honeycomb shards vertically. Spoon tonka air alongside. Garnish with cocoa nibs, espresso reduction, and micro mint. Finish with aromatic banana mist if available.
