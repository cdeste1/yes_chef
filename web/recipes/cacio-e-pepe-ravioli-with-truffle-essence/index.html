Cacio e Pepe Ravioli with Truffle Essence

Chef: Cerea Brothers
Source: adapted from Da Vittorio (Brusaporto, Italy)

Refined ravioli filled with Pecorino Romano and mascarpone, finished in a truffle essence sauce — a luxurious interpretation of Rome’s classic cacio e pepe.

Ingredients

  • Ravioli (see sub-recipe)
  • Cacio Pepe Filling (see sub-recipe)
  • Truffle Essence (see sub-recipe)
  • Shaved black truffle (for garnish)
  • Cracked black pepper (to finish)

Instructions

  1. Cut pasta rounds, fill with cheese mix, and seal to form ravioli.
  2. Spoon filling into the center of one round.
  3. Place a round on top and seal edges, making sure no air is trapped.
  4. Boil ravioli for 2–3 minutes until tender.
  5. Toss gently in truffle essence sauce.
  6. Arrange on plate, spoon over sauce, and garnish with shaved truffle and pepper.
