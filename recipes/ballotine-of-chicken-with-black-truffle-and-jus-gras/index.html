      Bresse Chicken Ballotine
      Ingredients:
      • 1 – Bresse chicken (about 3–4 lbs, skin on. If you cannot find a Bresse chicken, use a high quality whole chicken. Boneless skin on chicken breasts may also be used if deboning a chicken is difficult)
      • 150 g – Black truffle (fresh or preserved in brine)
      • 100 g – Unsalted butter (softened)
      • 1 tbsp – Fresh thyme leaves
      • Salt and white pepper
      Steps:
      1. 1.Debone the chicken, keeping the skin intact to form a flat piece of meat.
      2. 2.Prepare truffle butter: finely mince 50 g of truffle and mix with softened butter, thyme, salt, and pepper.
      3. 3.Spread truffle butter evenly over the flattened chicken. Place remaining truffle slices on top.
      4. 4.Roll chicken tightly and secure with kitchen twine or sausage netting.
      5. 5.Cook: either poach in a water bath at 160°F (70°C) for 30–40 min, or roast in oven at 350°C (180°C) for 35–45 min until internal temp reaches 150°F.
      6. 6.Remove from heat and rest 10 minutes before slicing carefully.
      Jus Gras (Rich Chicken and Truffle Sauce)
      Ingredients:
      • Chicken bones and wings (reserved from deboning.)
      • 1 small – Carrot (chopped)
      • 1 small – Onion (chopped)
      • 1 – Celery stalk (chopped)
      • 2 – Garlic cloves (smashed)
      • 3/4 cup – Dry white wine
      • 2 cups – Chicken stock
      • 4 tablespoons – Butter (cold, cubed)
      • 30 g – Black truffle (finely diced)
      • Salt and white pepper
      Steps:
      1. 1.Roast chicken bones, wings, and vegetables at 400°F (205°C) until browned (20–25 min).
      2. 2.Deglaze pan with white wine and reduce by half.
      3. 3.Add chicken stock, simmer 20–30 min. Strain and reduce to concentrate flavor.
      4. 4.Off heat, whisk in cold butter cubes to emulsify.
      5. 5.Stir in finely diced black truffle and season to taste.
