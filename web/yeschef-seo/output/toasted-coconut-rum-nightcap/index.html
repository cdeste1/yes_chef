Toasted Coconut & Rum Nightcap

Chef:
Source: Yes Chef! Kitchen

A warm, silky dessert cocktail with toasted coconut, dark rum, and vanilla.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Dark Rum
  • 2 ounces Toasted Coconut Cream (see sub-recipe)
  • 2 drops Vanilla Extract

Instructions

  1. Shake all ingredients with ice until creamy.
  2. Strain into a chilled rocks glass.
