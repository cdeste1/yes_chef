Rib-eye, mushroom purée and beef tea
Source: adapted from The French Laundry (Yountville, USA)
Inspiring Chef: Tom Keller

An elegant course, pairing beef with mushroom purée and potatoes cooked with bacon.

6 plates

Ingredients:

  • 4 1/2 lb – Rib-eye steak
  • 1 ounce – Butter
  • 2 – Garlic cloves (peeled and sliced)
  • 1/4 ounce – Thyme Leaves
  • enough to rub the steak – Extra-virgin olive oil
  • to season the steak – salt and pepper
  • Mushroom Purée (see sub-recipe)
  • Beef Tea (see sub-recipe)
  • 3-4 – Baby Carrots (a handful works)
  • 1 – Little Gem Lettuce (substitute spinach or arugala)

Steps:

  1. 1.Prepare the Beef Tea and Marcarie Potatoes
  2. 2.Prepare the Mushroom Purée
  3. 3.Blanch the baby carrots in boiling water for 3-5 minutes. Remove and put on ice to stop the cooking process
  4. 4.Peel the skin off the carrots, add 3 1/2 ounces butter and 3 ounces water}
  5. 5.Trim fat from the rib-eye.
  6. 6.Trim the meat surrounding the rib-eye and set aside.
  7. 7.Roll the trimmed meat in plastic wrap and cut into steaks.
  8. 8.Place the rolled steaks, garlic cloves, and thyme leaves into a plastic bag and seal.
  9. 9.Cook in a water bath at 117°F (47°C) for 4 hours.
  10. 10.Remote from the bath, scrape off garlic and thyme and cut the braised beef in half. Store in the refrigerator.
  11. 11.Season the rib-eye with salt and pepper.
  12. 12.In a hot pan with olive oil, sear both side of the rib-eye
  13. 13.Cut the braised beef into 1 inch pieces and add into the pan with 1 ounce of butter .
  14. 14.When cooked to your liking, remove from pan and rest
  15. 15.Peel the skin off the carrots and warm over low hear with 3 1/2 ounces butter and 3 ounces water
  16. 16.Cut the Little Gem Lettuce
  17. 17.Plating Layer 1: Place the Mushroom Purée on the plate. Place the braised beef pieces on the purée.
  18. 18.Plating Layer 2: Place the Marcaire Potatoes on the plate. Cut the rib-eye into long slices and place on the potatoes.
  19. 19.Plating Layer 3: Place the lettuce and carrots on the plate

Sub-Recipes:

Mushroom Purée
Ingredients:
  • 1 1/8 lb – Flat Mushrooms
  • 1 3/4 oz – Porcini Mushrooms (soak in 1/2 cup of water for 30 minutes, then allow to dry)
  • dash – Milk
  • 3 1/2 ounces – Butter
  • 2 – Garlic cloves
  • 1/4 ounce – Thyme
Steps:
  1. 1.Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C)
  2. 2.Place all ingredients into a pan, cover in foil and cook for 1 hour.
  3. 3.Purée and pass through a fine sieve.
  4. 4.Add the dash of milk and reheat on low-medium heat
Marcaire Potatoes
Ingredients:
  • 4 – Potatoes (large)
  • 2 tablespoons – Butter
  • 2 – Spring Onion (finely sliced)
  • 3 1/2 ounces – Bacon (cooked, finely diced)
  • 2 tablespoons – Extra-virgin olive oil
Steps:
  1. 1.Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C)
  2. 2.Bake the potatoes for about an hour, or until soft
  3. 3.Scoop the potato flesh and mix with the cooked bacon and spring onion.
  4. 4.Place the mixture into a 2 inch diameter, 1 inch high ring
  5. 5.When ready to serve, pan fry the mixture in butter and oil until golden on both sides
Beef Tea
Ingredients:
  • 4 1/4 cups – Beef Stock
  • 1/3 ounce – Thyme Leaves
  • 2 2/3 ounces – Porcini Mushrooms (soak in 1/2 cup of water for 30 minutes, then allow to dry)
  • 3 – Tomatoes
  • 1/3 ounces – Salt
  • 3 – Egg Whites
Steps:
  1. 1.Mix all ingredients in a pan and bring to a boil
  2. 2.Lower to a simmer and cook for 1 1/2 hours
  3. 3.Pass through a muslin cloth and discard remnants
