Garden Composition with Herb Vinaigrette

Chef: Alain Passard
Source: adapted from Arpège

A delicate arrangement of micro-vegetables and flowers dressed with a bright herb vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • 4-6 shavings Baby Carrots, thin shavings
  • 4 wedges Baby Turnups, wedged
  • 6 slices Micro Kohlrabi, sliced
  • 4 Pea tendrils
  • 6-8 strands Micro Corn Shoots
  • 6-8 leaves Micro Radish Greens
  • 6-8 leaves Micro Mustard Greens
  • 6-8 leaves Micro Arugula
  • 4-6 tips Watercress Tips
  • 4-6 small leaves Sorrel
  • 4-6 tips Chicory Tips
  • 6-8 leaves Micro Mizuna
  • 4 tiny fronds Bronze Fennel Fronds
  • 4-6 sprigs Chervil Tips
  • 6-8 leaves Micro Basil
  • 2-3 leaves Micro Shiso
  • 2-4 leaves Nasturtium Leaves
  • 4 Edible Flowers: Viola
  • 3 Edible Flowers: Borage
  • 6 Edible FlowersL Nasturtium Petals
  • Herb Vinaigrette (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Arrange micro mustards, micro arugula, sorrel, and pea shoots as a base layer.
  2. Add the second layer of baby carrot curls, baby turnip wedges, Kohlrabi slices, Chicory tips, and Nasturtium leaves.
  3. Final layer of bronze fennel, Chervil, micro basil, and edible flower.
  4. Spoon 1 tsp at a time of the Herb Vinaigrette to gloss. Do not drench
