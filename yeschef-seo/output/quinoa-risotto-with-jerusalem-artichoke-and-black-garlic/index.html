Quinoa Risotto with Jerusalem Artichoke and Black Garlic

Chef: René Redzepi
Source: adapted from Noma (Copenhagen)

A refined plant-based risotto featuring nutty quinoa cooked to creamy perfection, paired with roasted Jerusalem artichokes and umami-rich black garlic purée.

Ingredients

  • Quinoa Risotto (see sub-recipe)
  • Jerusalem artichokes (see sub-recipe)
  • Black Garlic Puree (see sub-recipe)
  • Micro herbs (for garnish)
  • Extra virgin olive oil (for finishing drizzle)

Instructions

  1. Spoon quinoa risotto onto the center of a warm plate.
  2. Arrange roasted Jerusalem artichokes elegantly on top.
  3. Add dots or a swirl of black garlic purée around the plate.
  4. Finish with micro herbs and a drizzle of olive oil.
