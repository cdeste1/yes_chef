Beef Tenderloin with Bone Marrow Sabayon
Source: adapted from L'Ambroisie
Inspiring Chef: Bernard Pacaud

A luxurious main course pairing perfectly seared beef tenderloin with a silky, umami-rich bone marrow sabayon and red wine reduction.

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • Beef Tenderloin (see sub-recipe)
  • Bone Marrow Sabayon (see sub-recipe)
  • Micro herbs (for garnish)
  • Flaky sea salt (for finishing)

Sub-Recipes:

Beef Tenderloin
Ingredients:
  • 4 (180–200 g each) – Beef tenderloin steaks (center-cut)
  • 2 tbsp – Olive oil
  • 2 tbsp – Butter
  • 2 – Thyme sprigs
  • 2 – Garlic cloves (crushed)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (freshly ground, to taste)
Steps:
  1. 1.Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. 2.Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  3. 3.Sear in a cast iron skillet coated in olive oil over high heat until well-browned on all sides.
  4. 4.Add butter, thyme, and garlic; baste for 1–2 minutes.
  5. 5.Transfer to oven and cook until internal temperature reaches 54°C (129°F).
  6. 6.Rest 5–10 minutes before slicing.
Bone Marrow Sabayon
Ingredients:
  • 2 large – Beef marrow bones (split lengthwise)
  • 3 – Egg yolks
  • 60 mL – Dry white wine
  • 1 tsp – Lemon juice
  • Salt (to taste)
  • White pepper (to taste)
Steps:
  1. 1.Roast marrow bones at 200°C (400°F) for 15–20 minutes until soft and releasing fat.
  2. 2.Scoop out marrow and strain through fine mesh to remove impurities.
  3. 3.In a heatproof bowl over simmering water, whisk egg yolks and wine until thick and foamy.
  4. 4.Slowly whisk in warm melted bone marrow fat to create an emulsion.
  5. 5.Season with lemon juice, salt, and white pepper.
  6. 6.Keep warm over a bain-marie until ready to serve.
Red Wine Reduction
Ingredients:
  • 250 mL – Dry red wine
  • 1 – Shallot (minced)
  • 200 mL – Beef stock
  • 1 tbsp – Butter (cold, for finishing)
  • 1 – Thyme sprig
Steps:
  1. 1.Sauté shallot in a small pan until translucent.
  2. 2.Add wine and thyme; reduce by two-thirds.
  3. 3.Add beef stock and reduce until thick and glossy.
  4. 4.Strain and whisk in cold butter just before serving.
