Black Truffle Old Fashioned

Chef: Giorgio Bargiani
Source: adapted from The Connaught Bar

A decadent Old Fashioned infused with black truffle and raw honey.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Bourbon
  • 1/4 ounce Black Truffle Honey Syrup (see sub-recipe)
  • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 1, expressed Orange Peel

Instructions

  1. Stir all ingredients over ice for 15–20 seconds.
  2. Strain over a large ice cube.
  3. Express orange peel and serve.
