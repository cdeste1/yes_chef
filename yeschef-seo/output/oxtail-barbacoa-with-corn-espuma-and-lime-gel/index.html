Oxtail Barbacoa with Corn Espuma and Lime Gel

Chef: Enrique Olvera
Source: adpated from Pujol

Slow-cooked oxtail barbacoa served with light corn espuma and bright lime gel for a modern, flavorful presentation.

Ingredients

  • Cooked oxtail (see sub-recipe)
  • Corn Epsuma (see sub-recipe)
  • Lime Gel (see sub-recipe)
  • for garnish Micro cilantro

Instructions

  1. Place shredded oxtail in the center of the plate.
  2. Pipe or spoon corn espuma around or over the meat.
  3. Add dots or small quenelles of lime gel for brightness.
  4. Optional: garnish with micro cilantro.
