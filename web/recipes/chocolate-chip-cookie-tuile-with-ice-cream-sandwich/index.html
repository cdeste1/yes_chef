Chocolate Chip Cookie Tuile with Ice Cream Sandwich

Chef: Thomas Keller
Source: adapted from The French Laundry (Yountville, USA)

A refined reimagining of the classic ice cream sandwich. The textures contrast beautifully: crisp, creamy, warm, and cold in perfect harmony.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate chip tuiles (see sub-recipe)
  • Vanilla bean ice cream (see sub-recipe, or choose your favorite Vanilla Bean)
  • Optional Sea salt flakes (for finishing)
  • Optional Chocolate shavings (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Place one tuile flat-side up on a chilled plate.
  2. Add a small scoop of vanilla bean ice cream in the center.
  3. Top with a second tuile, pressing gently to form an ice cream sandwich.
  4. Sprinkle with sea salt flakes or garnish with chocolate shavings if desired.
  5. Serve immediately, or freeze briefly to firm before serving for a crisp–creamy contrast.
