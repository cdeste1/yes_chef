Roasted Chicken with Wild Mushrooms and Sherry Reduction

Chef: Eric Ripert
Source: adapted from Le Bernardin (New York City, USA)

A refined take on classic roast chicken — perfectly crisped skin, tender meat, and a luxurious sherry reduction paired with earthy wild mushrooms.

Ingredients

  • Roasted Chicken (see sub-recipe)
  • Sautéed Mushrooms (see sub-recipe)
  • Sherry Reduction (see sub-recipe)
  • Micro herbs (optional garnish)

Instructions

  1. Carve roasted chicken and place slices or portions on warm plates.
  2. Spoon sautéed mushrooms alongside or beneath the chicken.
  3. Drizzle sherry reduction over and around the plate.
  4. Garnish with micro herbs for a refined presentation.
