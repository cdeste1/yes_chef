World-Class Chef-Inspired Avocado Toast
Inspiring Chef: Inspired by Gordon Ramsay & Bobby Flay techniques

Elevated avocado toast featuring creamy textured avocado mash on thick sourdough, topped with precision-cooked 63°C egg, confit cherry tomatoes, toasted pine nuts, black sesame, and microgreens. A luxurious brunch dish inspired by fine-dining techniques.

2 slices (serves 1-2)

Ingredients:

  • 2 thick slices (¾–1 inch) – naturally leavened sourdough bread (artisan bakery preferred)
  • 2 – ripe Hass avocados (perfectly ripe)
  • 1 small (20g) – shallot (finely minced)
  • 1 tbsp – fresh lime juice
  • 1 tsp – fresh lemon zest
  • 2 tbsp – extra-virgin olive oil (high-quality fruity variety)
  • 1 tbsp – fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 tsp – fresh mint (chopped)
  • flaky sea salt (Maldon) (to taste)
  • freshly cracked Tellicherry black pepper (to taste)
  • ½ tsp – red pepper flakes or Aleppo pepper
  • 2 – eggs (for 63°C sous-vide)
  • 4–6 – cherry tomatoes (for confit or fresh torched)
  • 1–2 tsp – toasted pine nuts or sliced almonds
  • 1 tsp – black sesame seeds (mixed with lemon zest and sea salt)
  • handful – microgreens (shiso or amaranth optional)

Steps:

  1. 1.Prepare thick, even slices of sourdough using a precision bread knife. Toast until deeply golden and crisp on the outside, chewy inside. Optionally torch edges lightly for char.
  2. 2.Make the avocado base: In a mortar and pestle, gently crush avocado flesh with minced shallot, lime juice, lemon zest, chopped herbs, olive oil, chili flakes, salt, and pepper to a creamy yet chunky texture.
  3. 3.Cook eggs using sous-vide immersion circulator at 63–65°C for 45–60 minutes for silky custardy yolks. Keep warm.
  4. 4.Prepare confit cherry tomatoes by slow-cooking in olive oil with garlic and thyme at low temperature, or lightly torch fresh ones.
  5. 5.Assemble: Drizzle toasted bread with olive oil and flaky salt. Spread avocado mixture evenly. Top with egg, confit tomatoes, toasted nuts, black sesame blend, microgreens, and a final drizzle of olive oil or aged balsamic.
  6. 6.Serve immediately while toast is warm and crisp.

Quick Confit Cherry Tomatoes
Yield: Makes enough for 2 toasts
Ingredients:
  • 8-10 – cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • ½ cup – extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 – garlic clove (smashed)
  • few sprigs – fresh thyme
Steps:
  1. 1.Combine ingredients in a small saucepan and cook over very low heat (around 200°F/93°C) for 20-30 minutes until tomatoes are softened but hold shape.
  2. 2.Cool slightly and use as topping. Store leftover oil for other uses.
