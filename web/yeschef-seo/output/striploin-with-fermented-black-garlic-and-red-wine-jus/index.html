Striploin with Fermented Black Garlic and Red Wine Jus

Chef: Wolfgang Puck
Source: adpated from CUT

An indulgent fine dining dish featuring buttery Wagyu A5 striploin paired with the deep umami of fermented black garlic and a silky red wine reduction.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Wagyu striploin (see sub-recipe)
  • Fermented black garlic purée (see sub-recipe)
  • Red wine jus (see sub-recipe)
  • for garnish Micro greens or chive tips

Instructions

  1. Slice the rested Wagyu striploin against the grain into thick medallions.
  2. Spoon a line or dot of black garlic purée onto each plate.
  3. Arrange the sliced beef elegantly beside the purée.
  4. Drizzle red wine jus around the plate for depth and aroma.
  5. Finish with micro greens or chive tips for freshness and contrast.
