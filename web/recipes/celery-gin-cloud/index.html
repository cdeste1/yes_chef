Celery Gin Cloud

Chef: Heston Blumenthal
Source: adapted from The Fat Duck

A light, vegetal gin cocktail topped with aerated celery foam.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces Aviation Gin
  • 1 ounces Celergy Juice
  • 1/2 ounces Dry Vermouth
  • Celery foam (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Shake gin, celery juice, and dry vermouth; strain into a chilled glass.
  2. Top with celery foam.
