Lobster Tortellini with Bisque Reduction and Lemon Foam
Source: adpated from Mirazur
Inspiring Chef: Mauro Colagreco

Delicate lobster-filled tortellini served with a rich bisque reduction and a bright lemon foam for contrast.

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • Lobster bisque reduction (see sub-recipe)
  • Lemon foam (see sub-recipe)
  • for garnish – Micro herbs

Steps:

  1. 1.Cook tortellini in salted water for 2–3 minutes, until just tender.
  2. 2.Place 3–4 tortellini in the center of a shallow bowl.
  3. 3.Spoon bisque reduction around the pasta.
  4. 4.Top with a spoonful of lemon foam.
  5. 5.Garnish with micro herbs, lobster coral oil, or edible gold for presentation.

Sub-Recipes:

Lobster Filling
Ingredients:
  • 2 – Whole lobsters (2 lbs total)
  • 1 tbsp – Olive oil
  • 1 small – Shallot (finely diced)
  • 1 tbsp – Butter
  • 1 tbsp – Crème fraîche or heavy cream
  • ½ tsp – Lemon zest
  • Salt and white pepper (to taste)
Steps:
  1. 1.Poach lobsters in salted boiling water for 5–6 minutes, just until cooked. Cool and remove the tail and claw meat.
  2. 2.Finely chop lobster meat and set aside.
  3. 3.In a small pan, sauté shallot in butter until translucent.
  4. 4.Add lobster meat, lemon zest, and crème fraîche.
  5. 5.Mix gently, season with salt and white pepper, then chill until ready to fill pasta.
Fresh Pasta Dough
Ingredients:
  • 200 g – “00” flour
  • 2 – Egg yolks
  • 1 – Whole egg
  • 1 tsp – Olive oil
  • Pinch – Salt
Steps:
  1. 1.Mix flour and salt, make a well, and add eggs and oil.
  2. 2.Knead until smooth and elastic, about 8–10 minutes.
  3. 3.Wrap in plastic and rest for 30 minutes.
  4. 4.Roll out thin sheets using a pasta roller.
Assemble the Tortellini
Ingredients:
  • 1 – Egg
  • 1 tablespoon – Water
Steps:
  1. 1.Whisk egg and water to create an egg wash.
  2. 2.Cut pasta sheets into 3 inch (8 cm) rounds.
  3. 3.Place a small spoonful of chilled lobster filling in the center.
  4. 4.Lightly brush edges with egg wash, fold over to form a half-moon, then bring corners together to form tortellini.
  5. 5.Place on a floured tray and cover lightly until ready to cook.
Lobster Bisque Reduction
Ingredients:
  • Lobster shells (from step 1)
  • 1 – Carrot
  • 1 – Celery stalk
  • 1 – Onion
  • 2 tbsp – Tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup – Brandy or cognac
  • 2 cups – Fish stock or water
  • 1/2 cup – Cream
  • 1 tbsp – Butter (for finishing)
Steps:
  1. 1.Roast shells and vegetables in a pan until caramelized.
  2. 2.Add tomato paste and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  3. 3.Deglaze with brandy; flambé or reduce until almost dry.
  4. 4.Add stock and simmer 30–40 minutes.
  5. 5.Strain and reduce to a thick, glossy consistency.
  6. 6.Finish with cream and butter for richness. Season to taste.
Lemon Foam
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup – Fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup – Water
  • 1 tbsp – Sugar
  • 1 g or 1 sheet – Gelatin (optional stabilizer)
Steps:
  1. 1.Mix lemon juice, water, and sugar. Warm gently to dissolve sugar.
  2. 2.Add gelatin if using.
  3. 3.Froth with an immersion blender to create foam just before plating.
