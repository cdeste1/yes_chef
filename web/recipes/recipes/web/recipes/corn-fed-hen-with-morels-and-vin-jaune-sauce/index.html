      Sub-Recipes:

      Corn-Fed Hen
      Ingredients:
      • 1 – Corn-fed hen (about 3 lbs, jointed or whole)
      • Salt and black pepper
      • 2 tbsp – Unsalted butter
      • 2 tbsp – Olive oil
      Steps:
      1. 1.Season the hen generously with salt and pepper.
      2. 2.Heat butter and oil in skillet or roasting pan.
      3. 3.Brown hen on all sides until golden.
      4. 4.Transfer to oven at 350°F (180°C) and roast 30–40 min until internal temp reaches 160°F (70°C).
      5. 5.Rest hen 10 min, then carve.
      Morels
      Ingredients:
      • 150 g – Fresh or rehydrated dried morels
      • 1 – Shallot (finely diced)
      • 1 tbsp – Unsalted butter
      • pinch – Salt
      Steps:
      1. 1.If using dried morels, soak in warm water 30 min and rinse thoroughly.
      2. 2.Sauté shallots in butter until translucent.
      3. 3.Add morels and cook gently until tender and fragrant.
      4. 4.Season lightly with salt and keep warm.
      Vin Jaune Sauce
      Ingredients:
      • Pan drippings from roasted hen
      • 50 g – Unsalted butter
      • 1 small – Shallot (minced)
      • 100 mL – Vin Jaune (or dry white wine if unavailable)
      • 150 mL – Chicken stock
      • 50 mL – Heavy cream
      • to taste – Salt and pepper
      Steps:
      1. 1.Deglaze roasting pan with Vin Jaune, scraping up browned bits.
      2. 2.Add chicken stock and simmer 5–10 min.
      3. 3.Stir in cream and reduce slightly to a glossy sauce.
      4. 4.Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
