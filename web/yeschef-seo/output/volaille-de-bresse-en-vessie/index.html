Volaille de Bresse en Vessie

Chef: Paul Bocuse
Source: adapted from Collonges-au-Mont-d'Or (Lyon)

Bresse chicken poached in a bladder, served with creamy sauce suprême and truffle.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bresse chicken (If you cannot find a Bresse chicken, use a high quality whole chicken.)
  • 1 Pig’s bladder (a sealable plastic bag may be used as an alternative)
  • 1 Truffle
  • Sauce Suprême (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Stuff the chicken cavity with truffle slices.
  2. Seal chicken inside bladder with kitchen twine. Folder over the top and tie again to make sure no air can escape.
  3. Place the sealed bladder in a large stock pot with chicken broth. Simmer for about 1.5 hours.
  4. Ladle broth over the bladder periodically to keep it moist.
  5. Serve carved chicken with sauce suprême and truffle slices.
