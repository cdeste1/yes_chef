Layered Garden Vegetable Terrine with Pea Puree, Glazed Roots and Parmesan Tuile

Chef:
Source:

An elegant vegetarian course, pairing zucchini, peas, and other delicious vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 Zucchini (Thinly Sliced Lengthwise)
  • 1 Yellow Squash (Thinly Sliced)
  • 1 Medium Beetroot (Peeled, Thinly Sliced)
  • 1/4 ounce Carrot (Thin Ribbons)
  • 1 tablespoon Extra-virgin Olive Oil
  • To Taste Salt and White Pepper
  • 1 Cup Fresh or Frozen Peas
  • 1 TBSP Butter
  • 1 TBSP Cream
  • 1 Splash Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Cup Baby Carrots
  • 1/4 Cup Pearl Onions
  • 1 Cup Wild Mushrooms (Oyster or Shiitake)
  • 1/2 Cup Parmsesan Cheese (Finely Grated)
  • 1 handful Microgreens
  • 5 Edible Flowers
  • To Taste Balsamic Glaze

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Vegetable Terrine
  2. Make the Pea Purée
  3. Glaze the Vegetables
  4. Make the Parmesan Tuile
  5. (Optional) Foam Element.
  6. Plate Like a Michelin Chef.
