Five Ages of Parmigiano Reggiano

Chef: Massimo Bottura
Source: adapted from Osteria Francescana (Modena, Italy)

A tribute to Emilia-Romagna’s king of cheeses, presenting Parmigiano Reggiano in five distinct textures and maturations — crisp, sauce, foam, wafer, and air.

Ingredients

  • Black pepper oil (for garnish)
  • Micro basil (for garnish)
  • Parmigiano Crisp (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Sauce (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Foam (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Wafer (see sub-recipe)
  • Parmigiano Air (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Spoon warm sauce in center of a shallow bowl.
  2. Add Parmigiano foam, crisp, and wafer.
  3. Top with Parmigiano air.
  4. Finish with micro basil and a drizzle of black pepper oil.
