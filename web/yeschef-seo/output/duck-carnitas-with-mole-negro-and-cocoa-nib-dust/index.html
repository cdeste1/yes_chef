Duck Carnitas with Mole Negro and Cocoa Nib Dust

Chef: Enrique Olvera
Source: adpated from Pujol

A refined twist on traditional carnitas — slow-cooked duck legs with a rich Oaxacan-style mole negro, finished with a dusting of cocoa nibs for bitterness and texture.

Ingredients

  • Duck Carnitas (see sub-recipe)
  • Mole Negro (see sub-recipe)
  • Cocoa Nib Dust (see sub-recipe)
  • Micro cilantro (for garnish)
  • 1 tsp Toasted sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions

  1. Spoon a layer of warm mole negro onto each plate.
  2. Top with crisped duck carnitas.
  3. Dust lightly with cocoa nib mixture for aroma and texture.
  4. Garnish with micro cilantro and sesame seeds.
