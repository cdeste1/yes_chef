Negroni Caviar

Chef: Giorgio Bargiani
Source: adapted from The Connaught Bar

A molecular take on the classic Negroni: Campari–gin caviar served over a chilled orange gel.

Ingredients

  • Negroni Caviar (see sub-recipe)
  • Orange Gel (see sub-recipe)
  • 1 slice Dehydrated Blood Orange (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Spoon a thin disk or teardrop of the orange gel on a plate.
  2. Carefully spoon the negroni caviar on top of the orange gel.
  3. Garnish with a dehydrated blood orange.
