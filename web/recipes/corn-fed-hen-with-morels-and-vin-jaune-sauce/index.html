Corn-Fed Hen with Morels and Vin Jaune Sauce

Chef: Yannick Alleno
Source: adapted from French Cuisine

Roasted corn-fed hen served with tender morels and a rich, nutty Vin Jaune sauce. Elegant poultry dish with earthy mushrooms and flavorful sauce.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Spoon a generous amount of Vin Jaune sauce on the plate.
  2. Arrange the carved hen pieces on the plate.
  3. Spoon morels around or on top of the hen.
  4. Garnish with fresh herbs and service immediately.
