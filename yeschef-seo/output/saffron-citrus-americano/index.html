Saffron Citrus Americano

Chef: Niko Romito
Source: adapted from Il Ristorante (Paris)

A bright saffron-infused aperitif with bitter orange and sparkling tonic.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Campari
  • 1 ounce Sweet Vermouth
  • 1/2 ounce Saffron Citrus Syrup (see sub-recipe)
  • 2 ounces Tonic Water
  • 1, expressed Orange Peel

Instructions

  1. Build Campari, sweet vermouth, and Saffron Citrus Syrup over ice.
  2. Top with tonic water.
  3. Stir lightly and express orange peel over the surface.
