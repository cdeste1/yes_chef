Tacos de Ceniza y Mar

A Michelin-level taco tasting main course inspired by Enrique Olvera and René Redzepi. Featuring 72-hour wagyu barbacoa glazed over binchōtan charcoal, blue corn tortillas, bone marrow charred onion crema, fermented pineapple kosho, salsa verde pearls, compressed watermelon radish, and black garlic ash. A balance of smoke, acidity, umami, fermentation, and theatrical presentation.

Serves 4

Steps:

  1. Prepare the adobo: Toast guajillo, pasilla, and morita chiles on a hot comal. Blend with charred onion, garlic, oregano, cumin, cinnamon, vinegar, and veal stock until smooth.
  2. Cook the wagyu: Vacuum seal wagyu short ribs with the adobo and cook sous vide at 149°F (65°C) for 48 hours.
  3. Rest and glaze: Chill wagyu overnight in its fat. Reduce cooking liquid into a thick glaze. Shred wagyu and caramelize repeatedly over binchōtan charcoal while brushing with glaze.
  4. Prepare tortillas: Mix blue masa harina, warm water, and sea salt into a smooth dough. Rest 30 minutes.
  5. Press and cook tortillas: Press tortillas thinly using a copper tortilla press. Cook on a cast-iron comal and finish directly over charcoal for blistering. Brush lightly with duck fat.
  6. Make bone marrow crema: Roast marrow bones until bubbling. Char onions over open flame. Blend marrow, onions, crème fraîche, and lime zest until smooth. Pass through a tamis and charge in an ISI whip.
  7. Prepare pineapple kosho: Ferment pineapple and habanero with sea salt for 5–7 days. Blend with yuzu juice until smooth and refrigerate overnight.
  8. Create salsa verde pearls: Blend tomatillos, cilantro stems, jalapeño, and lime distillate. Use reverse spherification with sodium alginate and calcium lactate to create caviar-like pearls.
  9. Compress the radishes: Vacuum seal watermelon radish slices with rice vinegar, hibiscus, sugar, and salt until translucent and vibrant.
  10. Make black garlic ash: Dehydrate black garlic, tortilla scraps, and pasilla chile completely. Pulverize into a fine ash powder.
  11. Plate the tacos: Arrange tortillas asymmetrically on a black volcanic stone slab. Fill with lacquered wagyu, pipe bone marrow crema, add radish ribbons, pineapple kosho, and salsa verde pearls.
  12. Finish and serve: Dust with black garlic ash, garnish with micro cilantro and lime blossoms, then briefly cover with mesquite smoke before serving tableside.
