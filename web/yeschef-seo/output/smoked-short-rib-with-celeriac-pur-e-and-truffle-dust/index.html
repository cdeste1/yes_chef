Smoked Short Rib with Celeriac Purée and Truffle Dust

Chef: Gordon Ramsay
Source: adpated from CUT

Tender smoked short ribs served with smooth celeriac purée and finished with a delicate truffle dust for aroma and presentation.

Ingredients

  • Celeriac Puree (see sub-recipe)
  • Smoked short ribs (see sub-recipe)
  • Truffle Dust (see sub-recipe)
  • Micro greens or chive tips (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Spoon celeriac purée onto plate as a base.
  2. Place smoked short rib slices or chunks on top.
  3. Lightly dust with truffle.
  4. Optional: drizzle some reduced cooking jus around for extra flavor.
  5. Garnish with micro herbs or chive tips for visual contrast.
