Elderflower–Bergamot Spritz

Chef:
Source: Yes Chef! Kitchen

A bright spritz of elderflower, bergamot cordial, and sparkling wine.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce St. Germain
  • 0.5 ounce Bergamot liquor
  • 3 ounce Prosecco
  • 1 ounce Club soda (chilled)
  • 1 dash Orange Bitters
  • 1 Bergamot peel (Optionally use a lemon twist)

Instructions

  1. Add ice to a wine glass.
  2. Add St. Germain and Bergamot.
  3. Top with prosecco and club soda.
  4. Add dash of orange bitters.
  5. Express bergamot peel and drop in glass.
