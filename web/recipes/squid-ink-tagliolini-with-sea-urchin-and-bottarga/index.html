Squid Ink Tagliolini with Sea Urchin and Bottarga

Chef: Mauro Colagreco
Source: adapted from Le Bernardin (New York City, USA)

Fresh squid ink tagliolini served with a rich sea urchin sauce, finished with grated bottarga and a drizzle of olive oil. A luxurious, delicate seafood pasta with bright lemon zest and optional herbs.

Ingredients

  • Squid Ink Tagliolini (see sub-recipe)
  • Sea urchin sauce (see sub-recipe)
  • 1 Lemon (for juice)
  • 20 g Bottarga (mullet or tuna) (finely grated or shaved)
  • Remaining sea urchin roe (for garnish)
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Chives or micro basil (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta in heavily salted water for 2 minutes, until al dente.
  2. Transfer directly to the sea urchin sauce with a splash of pasta water.
  3. Toss gently until the pasta is glossy and coated. Adjust salt and acidity with lemon if needed.
  4. Plate immediately, topping with remaining sea urchin roe, grated bottarga, a drizzle of olive oil, and optional herbs.
