Truffle Milk Bread Rolls

Chef: Thomas Keller
Source: adapted from The French Laundry (Yountville, USA)

Ultra-soft Japanese milk bread rolls infused with black truffle aroma.

Ingredients

  • 400 g Bread flour
  • 220 g Milk
  • 40 g Butter
  • 40 g Sugar
  • 7 g Yeast
  • 8 g Salt
  • 15 g Truffle paste
  • to shave Fresh black truffle (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, milk, butter, sugar, yeast, salt, and truffle paste; knead until elastic.
  2. Proof 1 hour; shape rolls.
  3. Final proof 45 minutes, bake 180°C (350°F).
  4. Brush rolls with melted butter and shave truffle over top.
