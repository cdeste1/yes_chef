Caramelized Onion Sourdough with Smoked Sea Salt

Chef: Daniel Humm
Source: adapted from Eleven Madison Park (NYC)

A rustic sourdough enriched with deeply caramelized onions and finished with smoked salt.

Ingredients

  • Caramelized onions (see sub-recipe)
  • Sourdough Bread (see sub-recipe)
  • as needed Smoked sea salt (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Cool and slice; finish with smoked salt.
