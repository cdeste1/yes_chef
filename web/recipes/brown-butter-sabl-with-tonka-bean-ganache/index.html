Brown Butter Sablé with Tonka Bean Ganache

Chef: Pierre Hermé
Source: adapted from Pierre Gagnaire

Crisp brown butter sablé cookies paired with a luxurious, aromatic tonka bean ganache for a balance of nutty, buttery crunch and creamy spiced chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Brown Butter Sablé (see sub-recipe)
  • Tonka Bean Ganache (see sub-recipe)
  • 2 tbsp Cocoa nibs (for garnish)
  • Edible gold leaf (optional, for presentation)

Instructions

  1. Pipe tonka bean ganache onto half of the sablés.
  2. Top with a second sablé to form sandwich cookies or serve open-faced.
  3. Garnish with cocoa nibs and a touch of gold leaf for an elegant finish.
  4. Serve slightly chilled or at room temperature for the ideal texture contrast.
