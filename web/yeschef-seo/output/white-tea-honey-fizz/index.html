White Tea & Honey Fizz

Chef: Javelle Taft
Source: adapted from Death & Co.

A light, floral aperitif built on white tea, gin, and bubbles.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce Gin
  • 1 ounce White Tea Honey Syrup (see sub-recipe)
  • 1/2 ounce Lemon Juice
  • 2 ounces Sparkling Water
  • 1, garnish Edible Flower

Instructions

  1. Shake gin, syrup, and lemon juice with ice.
  2. Strain into a chilled glass and top with sparkling water.
  3. Garnish with an edible flower.
