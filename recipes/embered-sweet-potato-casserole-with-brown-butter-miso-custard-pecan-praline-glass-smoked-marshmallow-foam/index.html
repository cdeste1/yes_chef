Steps:

  1. Prepare the sweet potato custard base.
  2. Spread the custard into a buttered ceramic casserole vessel and bake at 325°F (163°C) for 22–28 minutes until just set.
  3. Prepare the ember-roasted sweet potato cubes.
  4. Prepare the brown butter miso caramel.
  5. Prepare the smoked marshmallow foam.
  6. Prepare the pecan praline glass.
  7. Prepare the pickled apple pearls.
  8. Top the warm casserole with ember-roasted sweet potato cubes.
  9. Pipe smoked marshmallow foam asymmetrically across the casserole.
  10. Drizzle brown butter miso caramel around and over the casserole.
  11. Insert pecan praline glass vertically for height and texture.
  12. Finish with apple pearls, micro thyme, and smoked flaky sea salt.
  13. Cover under a smoke cloche and unveil tableside for dramatic presentation.

Sweet Potato Custard Base

Yield: Base for 1 casserole

Ingredients:

  • 4 large – Garnet sweet potatoes
  • 120g – Cultured butter
  • 90ml – Heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp – White miso
  • 60ml – Dark maple syrup
  • 3 – Egg yolks
  • 1 tsp – Smoked Maldon salt
  • 1/2 tsp – Ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp – Ground mace
  • 1 tsp – Vanilla bean paste

Steps:

  1. Vacuum seal sweet potatoes with butter and maple syrup.
  2. Cook sous vide at 185°F (85°C) for 2½ hours.
  3. Roast at 450°F (232°C) until skins blister and sugars caramelize.
  4. Scoop flesh and blend with cream, miso, egg yolks, spices, vanilla, and salt until ultra-smooth.
  5. Finish with browned cultured butter and reserve warm.

Ember-Roasted Sweet Potato Cubes

Yield: Garnish for casserole

Ingredients:

  • 2 – Sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp – Duck fat
  • 2 sprigs – Fresh thyme
  • to taste – Smoked sea salt

Steps:

  1. Cut sweet potatoes into uniform cubes.
  2. Cook gently in duck fat until tender.
  3. Char over binchotan charcoal on a Konro grill until lightly blistered.
  4. Season with smoked salt and thyme.

Brown Butter Miso Caramel

Yield: 1 cup

Ingredients:

  • 100g – Granulated sugar
  • 60g – Brown butter
  • 40ml – Heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp – White miso
  • 1 tsp – Bourbon

Steps:

  1. Cook sugar into a dark amber caramel.
  2. Carefully whisk in warm cream.
  3. Add brown butter, miso, and bourbon.
  4. Reduce until glossy and smooth.

Smoked Marshmallow Foam

Yield: Foam for garnish

Ingredients:

  • 150ml – Whole milk
  • 100ml – Heavy cream
  • 6 – Marshmallows
  • 1 – Gelatin sheet
  • pinch – Salt

Steps:

  1. Bloom gelatin in cold water.
  2. Heat milk and cream gently and melt marshmallows into mixture.
  3. Blend smooth with gelatin and strain through a chinois.
  4. Transfer to ISI siphon and charge with 2 N2O cartridges.
  5. Cold smoke with a smoking gun for 45 seconds before serving.

Pecan Praline Glass

Yield: Decorative shards

Ingredients:

  • 150g – Sugar
  • 50g – Glucose syrup
  • 80g – Toasted pecans
  • pinch – Flaky sea salt

Steps:

  1. Cook sugar and glucose to deep amber.
  2. Fold in crushed toasted pecans and salt.
  3. Spread thinly on silicone mat and cool completely.
  4. Shatter into dramatic shards before plating.

Pickled Apple Pearls

Yield: Accent garnish

Ingredients:

  • 1 – Green apple
  • 50ml – Apple cider vinegar
  • 30ml – Water
  • 1 tbsp – Sugar

Steps:

  1. Dice apple into tiny pearls using a melon baller or precision cutter.
  2. Combine vinegar, water, and sugar.
  3. Quick-pickle apples for 20 minutes before serving.
