Lobster Taco with Saffron Tortilla and Caviar

Chef: Enrique Olvera
Source: adpated from Pujol

Delicate lobster tacos served on saffron-infused tortillas, topped with luxurious caviar and garnished with micro herbs or edible flowers.

Ingredients

  • Saffron tortilla (see sub-recipe)
  • Lobster Filling (see sub-recipe)
  • 20–30 g Caviar (high-quality)
  • Micro herbs or edible flowers (for garnish)
  • Crème fraîche or aioli (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place warm saffron tortilla on plate.
  2. Fill with lobster mixture.
  3. Top with a small spoon of caviar.
  4. Garnish with micro herbs or edible flowers.
  5. Serve immediately to enjoy the contrast of warm tortilla, sweet lobster, and briny caviar.
