Lobster Tortellini with Bisque Reduction and Lemon Foam

Chef: Mauro Colagreco
Source: adpated from Mirazur

Delicate lobster-filled tortellini served with a rich bisque reduction and a bright lemon foam for contrast.

Ingredients

  • Lobster stuffed tortellini (see sub-recipe)
  • Lobster bisque reduction (see sub-recipe)
  • Lemon foam (see sub-recipe)
  • for garnish Micro herbs
  • for garnish Edible gold leaf
  • for garnish Lobster Coral Oils

Instructions

  1. Cook tortellini in salted water for 2–3 minutes, until just tender.
  2. Place 3–4 tortellini in the center of a shallow bowl.
  3. Spoon bisque reduction around the pasta.
  4. Top with a spoonful of lemon foam.
  5. Garnish with micro herbs, lobster coral oil, or edible gold for presentation.
