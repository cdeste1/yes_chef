Inception

Chef: Grant Achatz
Source: adapted from Alinea

A drink inside a drink

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Boulevardier (see sub-recipe)
  • 2 ounces Old Fashioned (see sub-recipe)
  • 1-2, for garnish Luxardo Maraschino Cherry
  • 1, for garnish Orange Peel

Instructions

  1. Fill sphere ice mold with water
  2. Freeze for 1 1/2 hours.
  3. Turn the mold upside down and freeze for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. Remove from freezer.
  5. Use a heated soldering iron, or a clean drill to create a hole in the ice sphere.
  6. Use a syringe to empty the water out of the ice sphere. Alternatively turn the sphere over to pour out the water.
  7. Freeze for another 2 hours or overnight.
  8. Remove from freezer and pour the Old Fashioned into the ice cube.
  9. Carefully place in a glass.
  10. Pour in the Boulevardier.
  11. Garnish with a Luxardo Maraschino Cherry and Orange Peel
