Saffron 75

Chef:
Source: Yes Chef! Kitchen

A golden, aromatic twist on the French 75 featuring saffron gin and champagne.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 ounces Saffron Gin. (see sub-recipe)
  • 0.5 ounces Lemon Juice
  • 0.5 ounces Simple Syrup
  • 3 ounces Champagne
  • 1 Lemon peel
  • for garnish Saffron threads

Instructions

  1. Shake gin, lemon juice, simple syrup over ice; strain into flute.
  2. Top with champagne.
  3. Garnish with lemon twist and saffron thread.
