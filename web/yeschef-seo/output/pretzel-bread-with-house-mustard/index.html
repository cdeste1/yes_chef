Pretzel Bread with House Mustard

Chef: Daniel Humm
Source: adapted from Eleven Madison Park (NYC)

Classic pretzel rolls served with a sharp, fermented house mustard.

Ingredients

  • 500 g Bread flour
  • 300 g Water
  • 30 g Butter
  • 7 g Yeast
  • 10 g Salt
  • 1/4 cup Baking Soda
  • Mustard Sauce (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Mix bread flour, water, butter, yeast, and salt; proof 1 hour.
  2. Dissolve baking soda in 4 cups of water. Chill to room temperature.
  3. Shape rolls; soak in baking soda bath for 30 seconds; bake 230°C (450°F).
  4. Serve warm with quenelle of mustard.
