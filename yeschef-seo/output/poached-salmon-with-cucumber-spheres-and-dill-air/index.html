Poached Salmon with Cucumber Spheres and Dill Air

Chef: Mauro Colagreco
Source: adapted from Le Bernardin (New York City, USA)

A delicate modernist seafood dish featuring gently poached salmon, refreshing cucumber spheres, and aromatic dill air for a light yet complex presentation.

Ingredients

  • Micro dill or fennel fronds (for garnish)
  • few drops Olive oil (to finish)

Instructions

  1. Place poached salmon at center of a warm shallow plate.
  2. Arrange several cucumber spheres around the fish.
  3. Spoon dill air gently over or beside the salmon.
  4. Finish with micro dill and a few drops of olive oil.
