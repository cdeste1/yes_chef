The Torch and Truffle Royale Burger

Chef:
Source:

A sous vide-perfected Wagyu-style patty, flame finished with a torch for a delicate, smoky crust, layered with melted cheese and silky caramelized onion puree.

Ingredients

  • 2 Wagyu or high-quality beef patties (6 oz each)
  • 2 Brioche Buns
  • 1 Salt & freshly cracked black pepper (To Taste)
  • 2 slices Comté or Gruyère cheese
  • 2 Yellow Onions (Sliced)
  • 2 tsp Butter
  • Splash Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1/2 Cup Mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp Truffle Oil
  • 1 tsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 Clove Garlic (Minced)
  • A few drops Lemon Juice
  • 2 Pieces Butter Lettuce
  • 2 Slices Tomato Slices
  • 1 handful Microgreens
  • To Taste Foie Gras or Sautéed Mushrooms (Optional)
  • To Taste Balsamic Reduction

Instructions

  1. Sous Vide the Patties
  2. Caramelized Onion Purée
  3. Truffle Aioli
  4. Sear + Torch Finish
  5. Optional Luxury Layer
  6. Toast the Brioche Buns.
  7. Structured Assembly
  8. Plate Like a Michelin Chef.
