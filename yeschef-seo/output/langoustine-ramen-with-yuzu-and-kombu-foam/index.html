Langoustine Ramen with Yuzu and Kombu Foam

Chef: Seiji Yamamoto
Source: adapted from RyuGin

A refined seafood ramen built on roasted langoustine shells, with bright yuzu notes and a light kombu foam.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces Ramen Noodles
  • 6 Fresh langoustines (shells reserved for broth)
  • Lagoustine broth (see sub-recipe)
  • Kombu foam (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Arrange noodles, add langoustine meat, pour broth, garnish with kombu foam.
