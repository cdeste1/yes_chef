Steps:

  1. 1.Fill sphere ice mold with water
  2. 2.Freeze for 1 1/2 hours.
  3. 3.Turn the mold upside down and freeze for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. 4.Remove from freezer.
  5. 5.Use a heated soldering iron, or a clean drill to create a hole in the ice sphere.
  6. 6.Use a syringe to empty the water out of the ice sphere. Alternatively turn the sphere over to pour out the water.
  7. 7.Freeze for another 2 hours or overnight.
  8. 8.Remove from freezer and pour the Old Fashioned into the ice cube.
  9. 9.Carefully place in a glass.
  10. 10.Pour in the Boulevardier.
  11. 11.Garnish with a Luxardo Maraschino Cherry and Orange Peel

Simple Syrup
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup – Sugar
  • 1 cup – Water
Steps:
  1. 1.Whisk together sugar and water.
  2. 2.Bring to a boil and then simmer until reduced by half.
  3. 3.Remove from heat and let cool. It should thicken to a syrup consistency.
  4. 4.Store in the refrigerator.
