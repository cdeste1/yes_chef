Golden Orchard Spritz

Chef: Giorgio Bargiani
Source: adapted from The Connaught Bar

A refined aperitif spritz highlighting pear, elderflower, and dry sparkling wine.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Pear Elderflower Base (see sub-recipe)
  • 3 ounces Dry Sparkling Wine
  • 1 ounce Soda Water
  • 1, for garnish Thin Pear Slice

Instructions

  1. Add Pear Elderflower Base to a chilled wine glass.
  2. Add sparkling wine and soda water.
  3. Stir gently to combine.
  4. Garnish with a thin pear slice.
