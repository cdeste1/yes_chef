Wagyu Truffle Hot Dog – Le Chien Chaud de Luxe

An ultra-luxury reinterpretation of the classic hot dog: A5 Wagyu beef sausage cooked sous-vide, nestled in a vacuum-smoked brioche bun, finished with foie-gras espuma, hand-shaved black Périgord truffle, Ossetra caviar, and smoked truffle aioli. A three-Michelin-star experience in one perfect bite.

4 plates

  • 4 × 6-oz – A5 Wagyu beef frankfurters (or sausages)
  • 4 – Premium brioche hot-dog buns
  • 150 g – Fresh grade-A foie gras (for espuma)
  • 1 large (? 40 g) – Black Périgord truffle
  • 30 g – Ossetra caviar
  • 2 – Egg yolks (for smoked truffle aioli)
  • 150 ml – Neutral oil (for aioli)
  • 2 tsp – Black truffle oil
  • 1 – Shallot (finely minced)
  • 30 ml – Cognac

  • as needed – Edible 24K gold leaf flakes
  • optional swipe – Aged balsamic reduction
  • for cold smoking – Applewood chips
  • to taste – Salt, white pepper, lemon juice

Steps:

  1. 1.Prepare the Foie Gras Espuma and Smoked Truffle Aioli
  2. 2.Prepare the Vacuum-Smoked Brioche Buns
  3. 3.Sous-vide the Wagyu sausages in the Polyscience circulator at exactly 60°C (140°F) for 45 minutes.
  4. 4.Shock sausages in ice water for 2 minutes, pat dry, then sear briefly with the Searzall torch or hot pan for Maillard crust.
  5. 5.Using precision tweezers and plating tools, begin assembly on a pristine white plate.
  6. 6.Spread smoked truffle aioli inside the warm smoked bun, nestle the hot sausage, and pipe a ribbon of foie-gras espuma from the iSi siphon.
  7. 7.Shave 5–6 paper-thin slices of black truffle directly onto the espuma using the Brevetti slicer.
  8. 8.Add tiny quenelles of Ossetra caviar with tweezers, microgreens, and 2–3 flakes of edible gold leaf. Optional: artistic swipe of aged balsamic.
  9. 9.Serve immediately with negative space for dramatic Michelin-style presentation.

Sub-Recipes:

Foie Gras Espuma
Ingredients:
  • 150 g – Fresh grade-A foie gras
  • 1 – Shallot (finely minced)
  • 30 ml – Cognac
  • 120 ml – Heavy cream
  • to taste – Salt and white pepper
Steps:
  1. 1.Sear foie gras with shallot until lightly caramelized, deglaze with Cognac.
  2. 2.Blend to silky smoothness in Vitamix, strain through chinois, season, and charge iSi siphon with two N?O cartridges. Refrigerate upright for at least 2 hours.
Smoked Truffle Aioli
Ingredients:
  • 2 – Egg yolks
  • 1 – Garlic clove
  • 150 ml – Neutral oil
  • 2 tsp – Black truffle oil
  • ½ lemon – Lemon juice
  • for Smoking Gun – Applewood chips
Steps:
  1. 1.Emulsify classic aioli base in high-speed blender.
  2. 2.Infuse with applewood smoke using the Smoking Gun (seal and shake twice for deep flavor).
Vacuum-Smoked Brioche Buns
Ingredients:
  • 4 – Premium brioche hot-dog buns
  • for Smoking Gun – Applewood chips
Steps:
  1. 1.Place each bun in a vacuum bag, fill with applewood smoke using the Smoking Gun Pro.
  2. 2.Immediately seal on chamber vacuum sealer and rest 20–30 minutes for smoke penetration.
  3. 3.Remove and lightly toast insides just before assembly.
