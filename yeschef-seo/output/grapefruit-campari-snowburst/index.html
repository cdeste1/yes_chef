Grapefruit–Campari Snowburst

Chef:
Source: Yes Chef! Kitchen

Frozen grapefruit–Campari granita layered with chilled botanical gin.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Grapefruit juice (Ruby-red or yellow)
  • 2 ounces Campari
  • 1 ounce Simple Syrup
  • 1 ounce Water
  • pinch Sea Salt
  • 1 Grapefruit Peel
  • splash Prosecco

Instructions

  1. Whisk grapefruit juice, campari, simple syrup, water and salt in a pan.
  2. Freeze pan for 2-3 hours.
  3. Scrape with a fork every 45 minutes to form granita.
  4. Scoop snow into a glass and top with prosecco.
  5. Express grapefruit peel and drop on top for garnish.
