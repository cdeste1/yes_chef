Duck Consommé with Soba and Shaved Truffle
Source: adapted from Tetsuya
Inspiring Chef: Nobu

A clear duck consommé served with soba noodles and garnished with freshly shaved black truffle. Slow-simmered for clarity and rich flavor.

Serves 4

Ingredients:

  • Duck Consommé (see sub-recipe)
  • Soba noodles (see sub-recipe)
  • Truffle Garnish (see sub-recipe)
  • Truffle Oil (a few drops)

Steps:

  1. 1.Reheat consommé gently without boiling.
  2. 2.Place cooked soba noodles in the center of warmed soup bowls.
  3. 3.Pour consommé around noodles carefully to maintain clarity.
  4. 4.Top with shaved truffles. Optionally, drizzle a few drops of truffle oil for aroma.

Sub-Recipes:

Duck Consommé
Ingredients:
  • 1 (3–4 lbs) – Whole duck (or duck carcasses from previous cooking)
  • 8 cups – Cold water
  • Egg whites (for clarification)
  • 1 – Onion (chopped)
  • 1 – Carrot (chopped)
  • 1 – Celery stalk (chopped)
  • 1 – Bay leaf
  • 2 – Thyme sprigs
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
Steps:
  1. 1.Roast or sauté the duck until golden (optional for extra depth).
  2. 2.Combine duck bones/meat with cold water in a large stockpot. Slowly bring to a simmer, skimming off foam.
  3. 3.Strain stock after 1–2 hours, reserving liquid.
  4. 4.For clarification, whisk egg whites and mix with cold stock. Heat gently, stirring, until egg whites coagulate and trap impurities.
  5. 5.Strain through fine cheesecloth. Season with salt and pepper. Chill if needed.
Soba Noodles
Ingredients:
  • 200 g – Soba noodles (buckwheat)
  • Water (for boiling)
  • pinch – Salt
Steps:
  1. 1.Cook soba noodles in salted boiling water until al dente, usually 4–5 minutes.
  2. 2.Drain and rinse under cold water to stop cooking.
  3. 3.Keep aside until plating.
Truffle Garnish
Ingredients:
  • 10–15 g – Black truffle
Steps:
  1. 1.Carefully shave truffle thinly just before serving using a small truffle slicer or microplane.
  2. 2.Reserve for final garnish.
