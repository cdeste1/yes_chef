Cacao Sablé with Fleur de Sel Caramel

Chef: Alain Ducasse
Source: adapted from: Classic French Bistro

A refined Parisian dessert combining a crisp cacao sablé base with a luscious fleur de sel caramel filling, a balance between bitterness, sweetness, and salt.

Ingredients

  • Cacao sablé bases (see sub-recipe)
  • Fleur de sel caramel (see sub-recipe)
  • Optional Chocolate shavings or gold leaf (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Place cooled cacao sablé rounds on a wire rack or plate.
  2. Spoon or pipe a layer of warm caramel over each sablé.
  3. Allow caramel to set slightly at room temperature.
  4. Finish with a light sprinkle of fleur de sel and optional chocolate shavings or gold leaf.
  5. Serve at room temperature to enjoy the textural contrast between crisp sablé and soft caramel.
