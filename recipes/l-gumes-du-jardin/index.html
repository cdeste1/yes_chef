Légumes du Jardin
Source: adapted from Arpège (Paris)
Inspiring Chef: Alain Passard

Seasonal vegetables, gently cooked to highlight natural sweetness.

Serves 2

Ingredients:

  • 600 g – Assorted farm vegetables (see sub-recipe for each season)
  • Vegetable Broth (see sub-recipe)

Sub-Recipes:

Vegetable Broth
Ingredients:
  • 20 g – Carrot tops
  • 30 g – Leek greens
  • 500 mL – Water
Steps:
  1. 1.Combine all ingredients and simmer 1 hour; strain.
Spring Vegetables
Ingredients:
  • 120 g – Baby Carrots (mixed colors if possible)
  • 80 g – Baby beets (gold and chioggia (pink and white))
  • 60 g – Peas
  • 60 g – Fava beans
  • 80 g – Baby turnips
  • 120 g – Asparagus
  • 30 g – Sorrel Leaves
  • 15 g – Edible Flowers
  • 30 g – Tarragon
Steps:
  1. 1.Toss baby carrots with oil and salt. Roast 20 minutes at 350°F (180°C).
  2. 2.Roast beets 40 minutes at 375°F (190°C). Peel after roasting.
  3. 3.Boil water. Drop peas in water for 20 seconds. Remove and put in ice bath. Dry before serving.
  4. 4.Boil water. Drop fava beans in water for 30 seconds. Peel skins. Serve warm or chilled.
  5. 5.Simmer turnips in butter and water for 10 minutes.
  6. 6.Steam asparagus for 5-8 minutes.
  7. 7.Leave sorrel leaves, edible flowers, and tarragon raw.
Summer Vegetables
Ingredients:
  • 150 g – Baby Zucchini & Blossoms
  • 100 g – Baby Pattypan Squash
  • 80 g – Haricots Verts
  • 80 g – Heirloom Tomatoes
  • 60 g – Corn kernels
  • 60 g – Fennel bulbs
  • 15 g – Basil
  • 15 g – Chervil
  • 20 g – Edible Flowers
  • 20 g – Nasturtium Leaves
Steps:
  1. 1.Grill zucchini 1 minute per side.
  2. 2.Roast pattypan squash 15 minutes at 375°F (190°C).
  3. 3.Boil salted water. Drop haricots verts in water for 20 seconds. Remove and put in ice bath.
  4. 4.Shave raw fennel bulbs.
  5. 5.Leave all other ingredients raw.
Autumn Vegetables
Ingredients:
  • 120 g – Heirloom Carrots
  • 120 g – Golden and Red Beets
  • 80 g – Baby leeks
  • 80 g – Celeriac
  • 60 g – Kohlrabi
  • 60 g – Fennel
  • 50 g – Rainbow Chard
  • 15 g – Tarragon
  • 15 g – Thyme
Steps:
  1. 1.Roast carrots 30 minutes at 350°F (180°C).
  2. 2.Roast beets 45-50 minutes at 375°F (190°C).
  3. 3.Bring vegetable stock to a simmer. Add leeks and cover for 15 min.
  4. 4.Sear fennel, 60 seconds each side.
  5. 5.Heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chard and wilt for 2 minutes.
  6. 6.Leave all other ingredients raw.
Winter Vegetables
Ingredients:
  • 120 g – Jerusalem artichokes
  • 100 g – Winter turnips
  • 80 g – Pearl Onions
  • 80 g – Cabbage hearts
  • 80 g – Salsify
  • 80 g – Carrots
  • 40 g – Kale micro-shoots
  • 10 g – Parsley
  • 10 g – Chives
Steps:
  1. 1.Roast artichokes 30 minutes at 375°F (190°C).
  2. 2.Simmer turnips in butter and water for 15 minutes.
  3. 3.Roast pearl onions and carrots for 30 minutes at 350°F (180°C).
  4. 4.Melt butter in a hot pan and wilt cabbage hearts for 2 minutes.
  5. 5.Simmer vegetable stock and add salsify for 10 minutes.
  6. 6.Leave all other ingredients raw.
