Smoked Chestnut Boulevardier

Chef: Angelo Sparvoli
Source: adapted from The American Bar at the Savoy

A deep, roasted take on the Boulevardier with smoked chestnut syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces Bourbon
  • 3/4 ounce Campari
  • 3/4 ounce Sweet Vermouth
  • 1/4 ounce Smoked Chestnut Syrup (see sub-recipe)
  • 1, expressed Orange Peel

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with ice.
  2. Stir for 20 seconds and strain over a large cube.
  3. Express orange peel over the glass.
