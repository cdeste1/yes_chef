Malted Barley Flatbread with Wildflower Butter

Chef: René Redzepi
Source: adapted from Noma (Copenhagen)

Primitive-style flatbread using malted barley flour, served with aromatic wildflower butter.

Ingredients

  • 150 g Malted barley flour
  • 150 g Bread flour
  • 200 g Warm water
  • 8 g Salt

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients into shaggy dough; rest 30 minutes.
  2. Roll into thin disks; cook in hot cast iron until blistered.
  3. Serve warm with soft wildflower butter.
