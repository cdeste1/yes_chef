Caramelized Vanilla Bean & Brown Butter Cinnamon Rolls

A Michelin-inspired laminated brioche cinnamon roll with caramelized brown butter filling, Ceylon cinnamon, citrus aromatics, and a mascarpone vanilla bean glaze.

12 rolls

Ingredients:

  • 500g – bread flour (high protein)
  • 60g – granulated sugar
  • 10g – fine sea salt
  • 10g – instant yeast
  • 4 – eggs (room temperature)
  • 120ml – whole milk (warmed to 95�F)
  • 200g – European-style butter (cold, cubed)
  • 1 tbsp – orange zest (finely grated)
  • 180g – unsalted butter (for brown butter filling)
  • 220g – dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp – Ceylon cinnamon
  • 1 tsp – freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 tsp – vanilla bean paste
  • pinch – Maldon salt
  • 200g – powdered sugar
  • 100g – mascarpone
  • 1 – vanilla bean (split and scraped)
  • 2�3 tbsp – heavy cream
  • pinch – salt

Steps:

  1. 1.Combine flour, sugar, salt, and yeast in a stand mixer.
  2. 2.Add eggs and warm milk; mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. 3.Gradually incorporate butter and knead until smooth and elastic (windowpane stage).
  4. 4.Proof dough at 75�78�F until doubled, about 90 minutes.
  5. 5.Refrigerate dough overnight for flavor development and easier handling.
  6. 6.Prepare brown butter by heating butter until amber and nutty; cool slightly and mix with brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, vanilla, and salt.
  7. 7.Roll dough into a rectangle, spread filling evenly, and fold into thirds.
  8. 8.Chill for 20 minutes, then repeat folding process once more for lamination.
  9. 9.Roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness and slice into 2-inch spirals.
  10. 10.Place rolls into a buttered baking dish and proof 45�60 minutes until puffy.
  11. 11.Bake at 350�F (175�C) for 20�25 minutes until golden brown.
  12. 12.Whisk mascarpone, powdered sugar, vanilla bean, cream, and salt to form glaze.
  13. 13.Glaze rolls while warm and finish with optional garnishes.
