Agnolotti al Limone

Chef: Massimo Bottura
Source: adapted from Modern Italian

Silky agnolotti served in a lemon cream sauce. The Limoncello makes it a little sweet to balance the savory ricotta

Ingredients

  • 4.5 grams All-purpose or '00' flour
  • 3 sheets Egg Yolk
  • 2 Egg
  • 8 ounces Whole Milk Ricotta
  • to taste Salt
  • to taste Ground White Pepper
  • 2 cups Garlic Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Lemon Juice
  • Limoncello Cream Sauce (see sub-recipe)
  • Parsley (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring 4 quarts water and 1 cup kosher salt to a boil over high heat.
  2. Mix the ricotta, 1 egg, salt, white pepper, Garlic Powder, and lemon juce with a fork.
  3. Spoon the ricotta mixture about 1/2 inch from the edge of the pasta sheet, about 2 inches apart.
  4. Fold the pasta sheet to cover the mixture and pinch the long edge.
  5. Press between each ricotta spoonful to make individual agnolotti
  6. Cut with a ravioli cutter
  7. Repeat for all pasta sheets
  8. Place in the boiling water for about 1 minute.
  9. Ladle Limoncello Cream Sauce into each bowl.
  10. Garnish with Parsley.
