Yuzu-Glazed A5 Wagyu Tataki with Uni-Miso Purée, Gochujang Caramel, Thai Basil Espuma, and Crispy Rice Crumble

An exquisite Michelin-star Asian fusion course featuring premium A5 Japanese Wagyu lightly torched and glazed with yuzu, paired with silky uni-miso purée, spicy-sweet gochujang caramel, airy Thai basil espuma, and textural crispy rice crumble.

2 tasting plates

Ingredients:

  • 300 g – A5 Japanese Wagyu ribeye or striploin (trimmed)
  • 2 Tbsp – Fresh yuzu juice
  • 1 Tbsp – Light Japanese soy sauce
  • 1 tsp – Yuzu kosho paste
  • 100 g – Fresh Hokkaido or Santa Barbara uni roe
  • 50 g – White shiro miso
  • 1 Tbsp – Dashi stock
  • 3 Tbsp – Korean gochujang
  • 2 Tbsp – Cane sugar
  • 200 ml – Full-fat coconut milk
  • 50 ml – Heavy cream
  • 1 large bunch – Fresh Thai basil leaves
  • 100 g – Cooked sushi rice (cooled)
  • for garnish – Micro shiso leaves, edible gold leaf, yuzu zest, toasted black sesame seeds

Steps:

  1. 1.Prepare the Uni-Miso Purée, Gochujang Caramel, Thai Basil Espuma, and Crispy Rice Crumble
  2. 2.Season the A5 Wagyu lightly with sea salt. Vacuum-seal with yuzu juice, soy sauce, and yuzu kosho.
  3. 3.Cook in sous-vide water bath at exactly 52°C (125.6°F) for 60 minutes.
  4. 4.Shock in ice water for 10 minutes, pat completely dry, and reserve bag juices.
  5. 5.Brush Wagyu with reserved juices and torch the surface evenly until deep mahogany crust forms (10–15 seconds per side).
  6. 6.Slice against the grain into 5 mm thick pieces.
  7. 7.Plating Layer 1: Place three precise dots of Uni-Miso Purée on the plate.
  8. 8.Plating Layer 2: Arrange torched Wagyu slices in a gentle sweeping arc. Drizzle with warm Gochujang Caramel.
  9. 9.Plating Layer 3: Dispense two elegant mounds of Thai Basil Espuma. Scatter Crispy Rice Crumble sparingly.
  10. 10.Finish with micro shiso leaves, edible gold leaf flakes, microplaned yuzu zest, and toasted black sesame seeds.

Sub-Recipes:

Uni-Miso Purée
Ingredients:
  • 100 g – Fresh uni roe
  • 50 g – White shiro miso
  • 1 Tbsp – Dashi stock
  • 1 tsp – Rice vinegar
Steps:
  1. 1.Combine all ingredients in the mixing bowl.
  2. 2.Blend at speed 8 for 2 minutes, scraping sides once.
  3. 3.Pass through a fine chinois strainer for silky texture. Transfer to squeeze bottle and chill.
Gochujang Caramel
Ingredients:
  • 3 Tbsp – Korean gochujang
  • 2 Tbsp – Cane sugar
  • 1 Tbsp – Rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp – Toasted sesame oil
Steps:
  1. 1.Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  2. 2.Stir until sugar dissolves and mixture reduces to glossy caramel (5–7 minutes).
  3. 3.Keep warm in bain-marie until plating.
Thai Basil Espuma
Ingredients:
  • 200 ml – Full-fat coconut milk
  • 50 ml – Heavy cream
  • 1 large bunch – Fresh Thai basil leaves
  • 1 tsp – Soy lecithin powder
  • pinch – Fine sea salt
Steps:
  1. 1.Blend coconut milk, cream, basil, lecithin, and salt for 60 seconds.
  2. 2.Strain through fine sieve.
  3. 3.Pour into iSi Gourmet Whip siphon, charge with two N?O cartridges, shaking vigorously. Refrigerate upright minimum 2 hours.
Crispy Rice Crumble
Ingredients:
  • 100 g – Cooked sushi rice
  • as needed – Neutral frying oil
Steps:
  1. 1.Spread cooled sushi rice thinly on silicone mat.
  2. 2.Heat oil to 180°C (356°F) and fry small clusters until golden and crisp (2–3 minutes).
  3. 3.Drain, cool slightly, and crush lightly into irregular crumbs. Hold warm until service.
