Saffron-Infused Cloud Soufflé Pancake
Inspiring Chef: Dominique Ansel

A molecular approach to brunch featuring vacuum-compressed berries, saffron-infused foam, and hyper-aerated soufflé structures.

4 plates

Ingredients:

  • 4 – Organic Egg Yolks (tempered to room temperature)
  • 6 – Egg Whites (chilled)
  • 50g – Cake Flour (sifted 3 times)
  • 40g – Castor Sugar
  • 1 tsp – Persian Saffron Threads (high-grade)
  • 1 – Vanilla Bean (scraped for seeds)
  • 250ml – Heavy Cream (for saffron foam)
  • 100g – Manuka Honey (UMF 20+)
  • 20ml – Aged Sherry Vinegar

Steps:

  1. 1.Prepare the Compressed Berries and Saffron Foam.
  2. 2.Bloom the saffron threads in 10ml of warm milk for 15 minutes.
  3. 3.In a copper bowl, whisk egg yolks with the saffron milk and vanilla seeds until pale and ribbon-like.
  4. 4.Gently fold the triple-sifted cake flour into the yolk mixture.
  5. 5.In a separate ultra-clean bowl, whisk chilled egg whites and castor sugar to a 'stiff-bird-beak' peak.
  6. 6.Using a silhouette motion, fold the whites into the yolk base in three stages to maintain maximum aeration.
  7. 7.Set the induction cooktop to exactly 165°C.
  8. 8.Place non-stick ring molds on the surface and pipe the batter until 3/4 full.
  9. 9.Add 5ml of distilled water to the pan and cover immediately with a heavy lid to create steam convection.
  10. 10.Cook for 6 minutes, flip with the offset spatula, and cook for an additional 3 minutes.
  11. 11.Prepare the Manuka Honey Gastrique.
  12. 12.Plating: Stack three pancakes in the center of a chilled stone plate.
  13. 13.Discharge a perfect quenelle of saffron foam from the iSi Whip onto the crown.
  14. 14.Arrange compressed berries and dehydrated honeycomb around the base; finish with 24k gold leaf.

Compressed Berries
Ingredients:
  • 200g – Wild Raspberries
  • 10g – Elderflower Syrup
Steps:
  1. 1.Place berries and syrup in a vacuum bag.
  2. 2.Seal in a Chamber Vacuum Sealer at maximum pressure to alter cell structure.
  3. 3.Chill for 20 minutes before serving.
Manuka Honey Gastrique
Ingredients:
  • 100g – Manuka Honey
  • 20ml – Aged Sherry Vinegar
Steps:
  1. 1.In a copper saucier, combine honey and vinegar.
  2. 2.Reduce over medium heat until it achieves a thick 'nappe' consistency.
Saffron Foam
Ingredients:
  • 250ml – Heavy Cream
  • 10ml – Saffron Milk
Steps:
  1. 1.Mix ingredients and pour into Gourmet Whip.
  2. 2.Charge with two N2O cartridges and shake vigorously.
  3. 3.Hold in a 4°C water bath.
