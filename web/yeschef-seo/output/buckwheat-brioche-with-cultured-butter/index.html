Buckwheat Brioche with Cultured Butter

Chef: René Redzepi
Source: adapted from Noma (Copenhagen)

A nutty, deeply aromatic buckwheat brioche paired with tangy house-cultured butter.

Ingredients

  • Brioche (see sub-recipe)
  • Cultured Butter (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Slice warm brioche and serve with quenelle of cultured butter.
