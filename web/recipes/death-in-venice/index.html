Death in Venice

Chef: Heston Blumenthal
Source: adapted from The Fat Duck

Cocktail that is refreshing and simply prepared

Ingredients

  • 1/2 ounces Campari
  • 4 drops Grapefruit Bitters (Orange bitters can also be used)
  • to fill the glass Prosecco

Instructions

  1. Add Campari and bitters to a champagne flute
  2. Top with Prosecco
  3. Stir lightly
