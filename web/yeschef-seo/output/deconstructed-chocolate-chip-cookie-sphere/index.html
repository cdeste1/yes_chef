Deconstructed Chocolate Chip Cookie Sphere

Chef: Grant Achatz
Source: adapted from Alinea

A deconstructed chocolate chip cookie in a crisp chocolate sphere with a molten or ganache liquid center. Served with optional cocoa, chocolate drizzle, or ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Cookie Dough Ball or Ganache (Choose one for the center of the sphere. See sub-recipe)
  • Chocolate Hemisphere (see sub-recipe)
  • Cookie Dough Ball (see sub-recipe)

Instructions

  1. Place chocolate hemispheres on a warm baking sheet to slightly melt edge.
  2. Place a cookie dough ball or ganache inside one chocolate hemisphere.
  3. Top with the matching hemisphere to form a complete sphere.
  4. Chill briefly to set.
  5. Plate: crack the sphere with a knife at the table for the deconstructed effect.
  6. Optional plating: dust with cocoa powder, drizzle with chocolate sauce, or add a scoop of vanilla bean ice cream or caramel foam. Or all of them!
