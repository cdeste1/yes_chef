Avocado Royale

An elegant avocado-centric brunch course featuring vacuum-compressed Hass avocado carpaccio, sweet Dungeness crab, a custardy 63°C sous-vide quail egg, and a light truffle-infused avocado espuma.

2 plates

Ingredients:

  • 2 large – Ripe Hass avocados
  • 100 g – Fresh Dungeness crab meat (cooked and picked)
  • 4 – Quail eggs (very fresh, room temperature)
  • 30 g – Fresh black truffle
  • 2 large – Egg yolks (for espuma base)
  • 150 g – Neutral oil or clarified butter (warm)
  • 1 tsp – Yuzu kosho (or fresh yuzu zest + pinch of chili)
  • 1 – Fresh yuzu juice (or Meyer lemon)
  • 20 g – Ossetra caviar
  • as needed – Micro shiso or chervil leaves
  • as needed – Edible 24K gold leaf
  • 1 tsp – Yuzu gel or reduction
  • to season – Fine sea salt and white pepper

Steps:

  1. 1.Prepare the compressed avocado carpaccio and avocado-truffle espuma
  2. 2.Sous-vide the quail eggs
  3. 3.Gently warm the Dungeness crab meat with a touch of yuzu juice and salt. Do not overcook.
  4. 4.Peel the sous-vide quail eggs carefully just before plating.
  5. 5.Season components lightly with salt and white pepper as needed.
  6. 6.Plating Layer 1: Place the compressed avocado carpaccio disc in the center of the plate using a 3-inch ring mold.
  7. 7.Plating Layer 2: Add a thin even layer of warm crab meat on the avocado base.
  8. 8.Plating Layer 3: Carefully place one peeled sous-vide quail egg on top of the crab. Remove the ring mold.
  9. 9.Dispense generous clouds of avocado-truffle espuma over and around the egg with the iSi siphon.
  10. 10.Spoon Ossetra caviar in an elegant line across the espuma. Shave remaining fresh black truffle generously over the plate.
  11. 11.Finish with tiny dots of yuzu gel, micro shiso leaves, and delicate flakes of edible gold leaf applied with micro tweezers.

Sub-Recipes:

Compressed Avocado Carpaccio
Ingredients:
  • 2 large – Ripe Hass avocados
Steps:
  1. 1.Peel and thinly slice each avocado into ?-inch rounds using a sharp knife or mandoline.
  2. 2.Arrange overlapping slices inside a 3-inch ring mold on the base of a vacuum bag. Top with another layer of slices to enclose.
  3. 3.Place in the chamber vacuum sealer and apply full vacuum + 2-second seal. Compress for 30 seconds.
  4. 4.Gently remove the ring mold. The avocado will hold a perfect glossy disc. Chill for 15 minutes.
Avocado-Truffle Espuma
Ingredients:
  • 2 large – Egg yolks
  • 100 g – Reserved avocado flesh
  • 150 g – Neutral oil or clarified butter (warm)
  • from 1 yuzu – Yuzu juice
  • 1 tsp – Yuzu kosho
  • 10 g – Fresh black truffle (finely grated)
Steps:
  1. 1.In a double boiler, whisk egg yolks with yuzu juice until pale and thickened.
  2. 2.Slowly emulsify in the warm oil to form a light base. Blend in reserved avocado flesh and yuzu kosho until silky.
  3. 3.Season with salt and white pepper. Grate 10 g fresh black truffle directly into the mixture.
  4. 4.Transfer to iSi siphon, charge with two N?O cartridges, shake vigorously 12–15 times. Keep warm in 50°C water bath.
Sous-Vide Quail Eggs
Ingredients:
  • 4 – Quail eggs
Steps:
  1. 1.Set sous-vide immersion circulator to exactly 63°C / 145°F.
  2. 2.Gently lower quail eggs (in shell) into the stabilized water bath.
  3. 3.Cook for 18 minutes for a tender white and custardy, runny yolk.
  4. 4.Shock briefly in ice water. Peel carefully just before plating.
