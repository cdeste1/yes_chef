Compressed Melon, Prosciutto Air, and Basil Pearls

Chef: Massimo Bottura
Source: adapted from Osteria Francescana (Modena, Italy)

A playful, modernist take on the classic prosciutto and melon pairing — featuring vacuum-compressed melon, a savory prosciutto-infused air, and delicate basil pearls for balance and freshness.

Ingredients

  • Compressed melon (see sub-recipe)
  • Basil Pearls (see sub-recipe)
  • Prosciutto Air (see sub-recipe)
  • Micro basil (for garnish)
  • Cracked black pepper (optional, for contrast)

Instructions

  1. Arrange compressed melon cubes neatly on chilled plates.
  2. Spoon basil pearls around or on top of melon.
  3. Add a spoonful of prosciutto air over or beside the melon for an aromatic finish.
  4. Garnish with micro basil and a light dusting of cracked pepper.
